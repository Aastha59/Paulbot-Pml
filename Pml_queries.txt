toll-free number "1800-102-1555" or email "info@paulmerchants.net". All services are provided under strict RBI compliance to ensure safety and reliability.
### International Money Transfer
You can easily receive money from abroad through services like USD, EURO, CAD, GBP at any Paul Merchants outlet. To collect the payment, you must present a valid government-issued 
photo ID, such as an Aadhaar card or passport. This service is completely "free for the recipient", and the funds are available instantly after the sender completes the transaction. 
### Foreign Exchange (Forex)
Paul Merchants offers foreign currency exchange for various travel needs, covering major currencies like USD, EURO, GBP, AED, and CAD. To purchase foreign currency, you will need
 to provide your passport, visa, and a confirmed air ticket. When selling foreign currency, a valid ID proof is required. Since forex rates change daily, it's best to confirm the
  current rates at a branch or by contacting customer support.
